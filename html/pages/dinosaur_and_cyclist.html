A Dinosaur and a Cyclist
Graphic Design / Animation

As part of exploration of animation, A Dinosaur and a Cyclist is a comedy sketch that shows an interaction between a man dressed in a T-Rex costume and a competitive road cyclist. It brings two distinct individuals together in a situation that I'm doubtful has ever happened before.

The scene takes place on the busy highway where a man dressed as a T-Rex chases a road cyclist. The animation ends ambiguously and leaves the viewer guessing whether the cyclist was able to escape the T-Rex.
