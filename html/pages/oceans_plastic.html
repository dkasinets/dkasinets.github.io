Ocean’s Plastic
Graphic Design

Every year huge amount of plastic finds its way into the Earth’s oceans. Once plastic gets there it disrupts marine environments. Fishes, sea turtles and sea birds often mistake plastic for food. We need to significantly reduce the concentration of plastic in the ocean to protect marine life.

Ocean’s Plastic is a call for sustainability education. Our decisions and actions need to consider the harm to the environment. Reducing plastic use or completely eliminating it is essential to slowdown global plastic waste crisis.
