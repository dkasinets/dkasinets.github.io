Ocean’s Plastic
Graphic Design

Every year huge amount of plastic finds its way into Earth’s oceans. Once plastic gets there it disrupts marine environments. Fishes, sea turtles, and sea birds often mistake plastic for food. We need to reduce the concentration of plastic in the ocean to protect marine life.

Ocean’s Plastic is a call for sustainability education. Our decisions and actions need to consider harm to the environment. It is necessary to reduce plastic use or completely end it. We need to slow down the global plastic waste crisis.
