Life of Cacti
Graphic Design

As a design challenge, I created Life of Cacti newsletter. It’s dedicated to growing beautiful cacti with flowers. It was an exercise to create a niche publication.

Life of Cacti introduces a viewer to Astrophytum, a genus of six species of cacti, native to North America. It also includes sections on how to grow, propagate, and repot the cacti.
